My 4-Week Beginner Plan

This is the plan I'm following. It's a 3-day split focusing on the main compound lifts to build a solid foundation. I train on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, with rest or light cardio on other days.

Day 1: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Overhead Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 2: Pull (Back, Biceps)

  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 5-8 reps
  • Pull-Ups (or Lat Pulldowns): 3 sets to failure (or 8-10 reps)
  • Bent-Over Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 3: Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes)

  • Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Leg Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets of 15-20 reps
