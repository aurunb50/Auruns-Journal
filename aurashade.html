Frequently Asked Questions

How do I measure my windows? +
We provide a detailed step-by-step measurement guide with videos. All you need is a steel measuring tape. You will measure the inside width and height at three different points, and we do the math for the deductions.
Do I need to hire an electrician? +
No! All our smart blinds are completely wireless, powered by an internal rechargeable lithium-ion battery. They install with just 2-3 screws into your window frame.
How long does the battery last? +
On average, a single charge lasts 6 to 8 months based on one up/down cycle per day. Recharging takes just a few hours using the included magnetic USB-C cable.
What is your warranty and return policy? +
We offer a 5-year warranty on the motor and a 3-year warranty on the fabric. If you aren't satisfied within the first 30 days, we accept returns—even on custom sizes!
