Wim Hof Method Breathing

!! IMPORTANT SAFETY WARNINGS !!

  • ALWAYS practice sitting or lying down in a safe environment.
  • NEVER practice this breathing technique in or near water (e.g., bath, pool, ocean) due to risk of fainting.
  • Do not force any part of the exercise, especially breath holds. Listen to your body.
  • Consult your doctor before starting if you have any medical conditions (e.g., cardiovascular issues, epilepsy, pregnancy, severe asthma).
  • Stop if you feel unwell or extreme discomfort.

Press Start to Begin

How to Perform (Simplified Guide):

1. Power Breaths: Take deep, full inhales followed by unforced exhales. Repeat for the selected number of breaths.

2. Exhale & Hold: After the last power breath, exhale fully (or let air out naturally) and hold your breath for as long as comfortable. Click "Recovery Breath" when ready.

3. Recovery Breath: Inhale deeply and hold for 15 seconds.

4. Repeat: This completes one round. Continue for the selected number of rounds.
